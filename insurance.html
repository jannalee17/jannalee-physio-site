Insurance & Direct Billing in Yorkton, Saskatchewan

We handle the paperwork wherever we can. For most major insurers, we bill directly — so in many cases you only pay any portion your plan doesn't cover.

Who we direct bill

Direct billing depends on your specific plan and the provider's rules. If yours isn't listed, ask us — we'll help you sort out your options.

How direct billing works

  • Bring your details. Have your insurance information handy when you book or arrive.
  • We submit the claim. Where your plan allows it, we bill your insurer directly after your visit.
  • You cover the rest. You pay only the portion your plan doesn't — often nothing, sometimes a co-pay.

Car accidents (SGI)

If you've been in a motor vehicle accident, SGI may cover your physiotherapy. You don't need a doctor's referral to start, and we can help you understand the claim process.

Workplace injuries (WCB)

Hurt on the job? We work with WCB claims and can bill them directly, so you can focus on getting back to work safely.

No coverage? No problem.

You don't need insurance to be seen here. We're happy to walk you through our fees up front so there are no surprises — just ask.

No referral needed. You can book physiotherapy or kinesiology directly — you don't need to see a doctor first.
A quick tip. Coverage limits and rules vary plan to plan. It's worth a quick call to your insurer to confirm your physiotherapy benefits — and we're glad to help you check.

Questions about your coverage?

Give us a call and we'll help you figure out what your plan includes before you book. No referral needed to get started.
