Recovering after a car accident

Car accident injuries are often more than they first seem. Whiplash, neck and back pain, headaches, and that wrung-out, achy feeling can linger if they're not addressed. The good news: in Saskatchewan, SGI may cover your physiotherapy, and you don't need a doctor's referral to get started.

Common after an accident

We help people recovering from:

  • Whiplash and neck pain
  • Back and shoulder stiffness or pain
  • Headaches that started after the accident
  • Reduced movement or trouble getting comfortable
  • Feeling 'off' or fatigued since the collision

How we can help

We meet you where you are and build recovery at a pace your body can handle — settling pain first, then restoring movement and strength so you can get back to your normal life. We'll help you understand the SGI process along the way.

What to expect at your first visit

Bring your accident and insurance details. We'll take a careful history, assess your movement, and start a plan. We can submit to SGI directly where your claim allows it.
