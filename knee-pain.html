Knee Pain Treatment in Yorkton, Saskatchewan

Stairs, kneeling in the garden, that ache after a walk — knee pain has a way of shrinking your world. We assess what's really loading your knee (often it's the hip or foot) and help you move freely again.

Book an Appointment

What's going on with your knee?

The knee is a bit of a middleman — it often takes the heat for what's happening above it at the hip or below it at the foot and ankle. So while we'll absolutely look at your knee, we're also movement detectives about everything connected to it. You might have a diagnosis on your knee, but that might not be the whole story.

Signs we can help with

We often see knee pain that shows up as:

  • Pain on stairs, squatting, or kneeling
  • Aching after walking, standing, or activity
  • A knee that feels stiff, swollen, or unstable
  • Pain with sport, running, or the gym
  • Discomfort that's slowly shrinking what you'll do

How we can help

We blend hands-on work with movement and strength — and our kinesiologists can help you build the hip and leg strength that protects the knee long-term. The aim is to get you back on the stairs, in the garden, and out walking without thinking about it.

What to expect at your first visit

We'll get the story, watch how you move, and assess the knee and everything that loads it. You'll leave with a clear plan that fits your life.
