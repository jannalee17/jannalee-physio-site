Recovering from a workplace injury

Workplace injuries range from a sudden lift gone wrong to the slow build of doing the same task all day, every day. Whatever the job — farm, trade, office, or shop floor — we assess what's hurt and what set it up, then build a plan that gets you back to work without rushing it.

Signs we can help with

We help workers with things like:

  • Back, neck, and shoulder injuries from lifting or repetitive work
  • Strains and sprains that happened on the job
  • Pain that flares with specific work tasks
  • Needing to return to work safely and at the right pace
  • Building strength and tolerance for the demands of your job

How we can help

We focus on both recovery and return-to-work — restoring movement and strength while keeping the demands of your actual job in mind. We'll work with your WCB claim and keep things moving.

What to expect at your first visit

Bring your claim and injury details. We'll take a history, assess your movement, and start a plan. We can submit to WCB directly where your claim allows it.
