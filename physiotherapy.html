What is physiotherapy?

Physiotherapy is hands-on, evidence-informed care for how your body moves. A Registered Physiotherapist assesses where your pain or limitation is coming from, then works with you to restore movement, build strength, and get you back to the things that matter — gardening, walking, lifting grandkids, getting through a workday without bracing for it.

Our approach is a little different. We're movement detectives: the spot that hurts is often not the spot that's causing the problem. So we look at the whole picture — how you move, how you load, what your day actually asks of your body — and treat the cause, not just the symptom. You might have a diagnosis, but that might not be what's causing your pain.

How we can help

People come to us working through things like:

  • Back, neck, hip, knee, and shoulder pain
  • Sports injuries and overuse strains
  • Car accident and workplace injury rehab (SGI & WCB)
  • Arthritis and stiff, aching joints
  • Concussion, dizziness, and vestibular issues
  • Jaw and TMJ pain
  • Pre- and post-surgical rehabilitation

What to expect at your first visit

Your first appointment is mostly about getting the full story. We'll talk through what's going on, what you've already tried, and what you want to get back to. Then we'll watch how you move and do a hands-on assessment to find where the problem is actually coming from.

By the end, you'll have a clear picture of what we think is going on and a plan that fits your life — not a generic handout. We move through three stages as you progress: Onboarding (getting movement back and pain down), Building (integrating it back into daily life), and Maintenance (a check-in to keep you there).

Who benefits

This might sound like you

"I've tried everything and nothing works"

Massage, chiro, rest, Advil — and it keeps coming back. Often that's a sign the real driver hasn't been found yet. That's exactly what we go looking for.

You want to avoid surgery

Surgery isn't always the only road. We'll work toward restoring movement and strength so you can see how far conservative care can take you first.

You're an active adult who got sidelined

A tweak in the garden, a sport injury, a flare that won't quit. You don't have to stop what you love — sometimes we just have to modify while it settles.

You were in a car accident or hurt at work

We handle SGI and WCB claims and direct billing, so you can focus on recovering instead of paperwork.
