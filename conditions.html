How we approach it

Whatever's on the list above, our starting point is the same: we listen, we watch how you move, and we go looking for what's actually driving the problem — which often isn't where you feel it. You might have a diagnosis, but that might not be the whole story.

From there we build a plan around your goals and your life. Hands-on treatment, movement, and a few simple things to do at home that fit your week. As pain settles, we shift toward strength and confidence so you can get back to what you love — and stay there.

We can't promise a specific outcome — nobody honestly can — but we can promise to take you seriously and work toward your goals with you. No referral needed to get started.
