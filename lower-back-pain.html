Lower Back Pain Treatment in Yorkton, Saskatchewan

Lower back pain is one of the most common reasons people come to see us in Yorkton — and one of the most misunderstood. We look beyond the sore spot to find what's actually driving it, then build a plan to help you move well again.

Book an Appointment

What's going on with your lower back?

Lower back pain can come from a sudden tweak lifting a bag of feed, a slow ache that built up over years at a desk or in a tractor seat, or stiffness that shows up after gardening. Here's the thing — you might have a diagnosis, but that might not be what's causing your pain. The back is often where you feel it, not where the problem starts.

That's why we assess the whole picture: how your hips and core move, how you load through a day, and what your body is actually being asked to do.

Signs we can help with

People come to us with back pain that shows up as:

  • Stiffness or ache after sitting, standing, or first thing in the morning
  • Pain that flares with bending, lifting, or twisting
  • A 'caught' or locked feeling in the low back
  • Pain that's been hanging around for weeks, months, or years
  • Discomfort that travels into the hip or buttock

How we can help

We can't promise a specific outcome — nobody honestly can — but we can promise to listen, hunt for the real driver, and work toward your goals. Treatment usually blends hands-on work, movement, and a few simple things to do at home that actually fit your week. As pain settles, we build strength so it's less likely to come back.

What to expect at your first visit

Your first visit is mostly about getting the full story — what's going on, what you've already tried, and what you want to get back to. Then we watch how you move and do a hands-on assessment. You'll leave with a clear picture and a plan built around your life, not a generic handout.
