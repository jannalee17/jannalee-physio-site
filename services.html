Our Services in Yorkton, Saskatchewan

Physiotherapy, kinesiology, custom orthotics, and group exercise classes — all under one roof. Whatever stage you're at, we'll help you work toward moving well and getting back to what you love. No referral needed.

Physiotherapy

Hands-on, evidence-informed assessment and treatment for pain, injury, and movement. We look beyond the sore spot to find what's actually driving it.

Explore physiotherapy

Kinesiology

Movement coaching and exercise programming to build strength, balance, and confidence — so the gains from treatment actually stick.

Explore kinesiology

Custom Orthotics

Custom-cast orthotics that support how you stand, walk, and move — moulded to your own foot, not pulled off a shelf. Sometimes knee, hip, or back pain starts right at your feet.

Ask us about orthotics

Group Exercise Classes

Kinesiology-led group classes that keep you strong, steady, and connected. Age Defying Fitness for adults 55+, and After Hours Fitness in the evenings for busy schedules.
